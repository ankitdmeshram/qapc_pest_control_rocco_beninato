Raccoons, despite their adorable look, can be extremely destructive, causing significant structural damage to your property. Raccoons are known for getting into garbage cans and making a lot of noise at night when they're most active. Worse, they leave behind excrement that could contain parasites, and they carry diseases like distemper and rabies, which can be transmitted to humans and pets if bitten. This is why raccoon removal in York Region and Durham Region should be left to professionals with experience in dealing with successful wildlife management!
